Water is life:
Protecting our most precious Resource
Worldwide, 2.2 billion people still lack access to safe drinking water.

1 in 2

Nearly half of the global population does not have access to safe sanitation.

419 million people practice open defecation.
1.7 billion people drink water contaminated with feces.
This contamination poses severe health risks, including diseases such as cholera, dysentery, typhoid, and polio.
Every 2 minutes, a child dies from a water-related disease.
785 million people lack access to clean water.
Clean water is essential for life, yet many communities around the world face severe water shortages and contamination issues.
